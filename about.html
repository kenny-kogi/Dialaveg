General Infomation

Dial-a-Veg was established in 2020 Nairobi, Kenya. We are one of the bright futuristic goal-oriented e-commerce stores that partners with your neighbourhood kiosks that provide you with your bassic everyday grocery needs.

We provide high quality products, helpful, stress-free customer service to the hospitality and associated industries.We supply homes estates and residential blocks but not limited to any other individual looking to get their products on the go.

Our modern fleet of delivery boda bodas ensure that the freshness of the product is not lost while in transit we have supplies networks throughout Nairobi city with area concetration such as Langata, Rongai, Karen, Westalands, Kilimani and many more.

The Process

We have a simple process that is convinient to follow for all individuals.

You just order the product you need, add them to your cart and check out. The checkout process is simple and convinient for all as we accept all modes of payement. The product then get delivered at your doorstep in as limited time as possible.
